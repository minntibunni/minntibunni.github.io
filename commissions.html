process

soft style commissions

i will send you a stripe invoice each time you need to pay. usually i can finish the commission within 2 weeks. during the process, i will send you updates on the art and what stage it is at (sketch, lineart, flat colours, etc).

  1. you pay a non-refundable deposit of 50% upfront. (100% upfront for roblox commissions)
  2. i send you a heavily-watermarked sketch.
  3. you pay the remaining balance. (n/a for roblox commissions)
  4. i send you the finished piece though dropbox (in PNG format).


no physical product is delivered.


pixel style commissions

the commission will usually be finished within a week. there will be no updates on what stage the art is at as it is a surprise-me.

  1. you pay the price you stated.
  2. i send you the finished piece in PNG and GIF format. (both transparent and opaque backgrounds will be provided)


no physical product is delivered.

privacy policy

("i" and "me" refers to the artist, minntibunni, who is providing the commission service. "you" refers to the commissioner paying for the service)


when ordering a commission i have to collect these data from you: your contact information, name/username and information about the commission that you are ordering. this is to ensure that i will be able to collect payment and complete and send you the finished commission. other information that i will be able to see are payment details through stripe if you use a credit/debit card such as the last 4 digits of the card number (not the full number as that is hidden from me), expiry date and your post code (not the full address as only the post code can be entered in the payment form). this is so that i get paid for the commission. data about the commission is stored on a google sheet and payment details are stored in my stripe account which are both protected through two factor authentication. the only information that may be shared to the public is your name/username if i share the commission on social media. the rest of the information won't be shared with anyone else.
